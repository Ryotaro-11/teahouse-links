Reinterpreting
TEAHOUSE UTSUSHI
with Artificial
Intelligence

This project reinterprets the traditional Japanese tea house "utsushi" (copying) culture through AI-generated architecture, using ThinkDiffusion to generate elevations trained on Kyoto tea houses while maintaining the structural framework of Konnichian's "okoshi-ezu" (development drawing). The generated tea house will be actually constructed at a university festival, with materials determined through collages created using Gemini's nanobanana to apply contemporary affordable materials to the elevations.
